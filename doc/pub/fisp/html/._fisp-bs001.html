Strategic importance

The aim is to lay the foundation for the development of new activities in Computational Science and Physics at the University of Oslo, with a strong focus on computational technologies for the future, from quantum computing to machine learning.

  1. The proposed activities are expected to lead to an application for a future center of excellence (The Norwegian SFF program) in Computational Physics and Science application, Computing Across the Disciplines, where focus on new computational approaches for studying complex systems will play a central role.
  2. Establish an educational
Marie-Curie training program on Computational Science and Physics,
  1. Strong links with the newly established center of excellence in education, Center for Computing in Science Education
